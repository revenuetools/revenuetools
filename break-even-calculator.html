Profit per unit
—
Price − variable cost
Break-even units
—
Fixed costs ÷ profit per unit
Revenue needed
—
Units × price
Margin
—
Profit per unit ÷ price
Note: If variable cost is equal to or higher than price, break-even isn’t possible with these values.
