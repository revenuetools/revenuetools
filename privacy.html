Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

CalcStack.tools provides free online calculators. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. We collect as little as possible.

Calculator Data

All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or process any numbers you enter. No calculation data ever leaves your device.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on site, general location by country). This data is anonymised and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) and advertising cookies (Google AdSense). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and Cloudflare (hosting). Each has their own privacy policy.

Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data depending on your location. Most requests can be addressed by clearing browser cookies. Contact us via the About page for queries.

Children

CalcStack.tools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
